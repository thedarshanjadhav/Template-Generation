Overview

The Eastern Express Highway provides access to Ruparel Celestia Mulund East, which has been updated to Ruparel Mulund East. It also has good connectivity through the Central Railway and the upcoming metro. This suburb is connected to Navi Mumbai and the Eastern Express Highway by the Mulund-Airoli bridge. Improved connectivity is anticipated as a result of the currently under construction Line 4 Mumbai Metro project, which will run from Wadala to Mulund.
